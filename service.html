Clinical Lab Quality Management System (cQMS)

Do you have scattered paper, loose binders, and piles of clutter papers in the lab? Do you struggle to find IFU and MSDS when needed? Does your lab struggle to show required compliance documents at the time inspection? Do you keep track of Lab director’s oversight, CAPA, and instrument maintenance records. We have you covered.

Our digital platform allows you to keep track of all laboratory activities online. Your lab is paperless. You can access laboratory compliance documentation remotely from anywhere. It gives a piece of mind from compliance inspection and you can focus on the business. If you enable our AI inspector agent, you can do a mock CAP/COLA/CLIA inspection anytime.

Clinical Lab Reporting System (cLRS)

Our lab reporting system is a simple and user-friendly tool to report patients. It is a HIPPA compliance software that allows patient reporting a peace of mind. It can be integrated to any EMRS available in the market.

Levey Jennings Tool: Real-time review of QC results on a daily basis. Automatically compares today’s QC result with past five results and gives you an acceptance decision based on WEST GUARD RULES.

Pharmacogenetics (PGx) Reporting

Our platform allows molecular diagnostics labs to upload sequencing data and create informative PGx reports. We utilize vast research articles and clinical trial data.

Reviewed by board certified Toxicologists/Pharmacologists and Pharmacists to derive professional review notes.

Cancer genetics (CGx) Reporting

We apply AI predictive models to interpret the clinical utility of SNPs related to cancer genetics, neurology, and molecular genetics.

Reports are vetted by board certified professionals of relevant biomedical fields before physician release.

Bioinformatics and Predictive modeling

We are a team of scientists who specialise in data science/machine learning, epidemiology, biomedical sciences and laboratory medicine. Our team of experts can be a part of your research team whether it is a drug development project, drug delivery project, basic molecular research or chemical risk assessment.
